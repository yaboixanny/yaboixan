# Blog Content Rules

Whenever creating a NEW blog post or updating existing content for the Yaboixan blog, you MUST follow these guidelines:

## 1. Structure & Formatting
All blog posts must strictly adhere to the outline defined in the [Blog Template](file:///Users/alexander/Documents/yaboixanblogAG/rules/antigravity_blog_template.md):
- **H1 Header**: Benefit-driven and SEO-optimized.
- **TL;DR**: Mandatory summary at the top.
- **H2 Sections**: Use the "Problem/Hook" and "Core Strategy" flow.
- **Short Paragraphs**: No more than 3 sentences per paragraph.
- **Micro-Animations**: If adding new UI components for a post, ensure they align with the site's rich aesthetic.

## 2. Tone & Voice
- **Direct & Punchy**: Avoid fluff. Start with the "why."
- **Expert Perspective**: Include "Pro Tips" and "Inside Baseball" insights.
- **Rugged & Authentic**: Maintain the "Canadian Roots. Ukrainian Soul." persona.

## 3. SEO Requirements
- **Keywords**: Ensure the primary keyword is in the H1 and at least one H2.
- **Slugs**: Use the automated slug system in `cms-server.js` or provide a descriptive, hyphenated slug.
- **Images**: Always include `alt` text and descriptive captions.
